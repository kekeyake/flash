gmp_von Gerkan, Marg & Partne Meinhard von Gerkan
Prof. Dr. h.c. Dipl.-Ing. Architekt BDA
born on January 3rd, 1935 in Riga/Latvia.





1964

ㆍDiploma in Architecture at the University Carolo-Wilhelmina at Braunschweig.

1965

ㆍFreelance architect together with Volkwin Marg.
ㆍMore than 200 completed buildings. More than 370 national and international competition awards together with Volkwin Marg, incl. more than 170 1st prizes.
ㆍNumerous awards for outstanding buildings. Numerous publications in Germany and abroad.
ㆍ Considerable involvement in competition juries and feasibility studies.

1972

ㆍAppointment to the Free Academy of Arts, Hamburg.

1974

ㆍAppointment to the University Carolo-Wilhelmina at Braunschweig as professor/chair A for design. Director of the Institute for Architectural Design A

1982

ㆍAppointment to the board of the Jugen-Ponto-Foundation, Frankfurt.

1988

ㆍGuest professor at the Nihon University, Tokyo/Japan.

1993

ㆍGuest professor at the University of Pretoria/South Africa.

1995

ㆍAmerican Institute of Architects, Honorary Fellow, USA.
ㆍHonoured by the Mexican Architectural Society. 

2000

ㆍRecipient of the Fritz-Schumacher-Award.

2002

ㆍHonorary Member of the Berlin-Brandenburg Academy of Sciences.
ㆍRecipient of the Romanian National Award. 
ㆍRecipient of the Honorary Doctorate by the Faculty of Protestant Theology at the Philipps University, Marburg.

2003

ㆍMember of Convent Chairmanschip of the "Bundesstiftung Baukultur", Berlin.
